Prounciation tricks

Over the years, I have developed a set of tricks to help non-chinese speakers prounce my name. Feel free to pick one that works for you the best!

  • Prounciation audio clip (last name comes first)

"Ze" can be prounced as "ch" or "Z" and "Yuan" shares the same prouncation with Chinese currency unit: "Yuan" (the chinese spelling is not the same though). I find this trick works particularly well in the econ department.
